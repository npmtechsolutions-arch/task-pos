# ProjectFlow - Welcome & User Guide

Welcome to **ProjectFlow**, our enterprise-grade project management and team collaboration platform! 

This guide will walk you through the core features of ProjectFlow so you can start managing projects, organizing tasks, and collaborating with your team right away.

---

## 1. Getting Started & Logging In

Your administrator should have provided you with a login account. If you are exploring the demo environment, you can use the following credentials:
- **Email:** admin@projectflow.com
- **Password:** admin123

Once you log in, you will be greeted by the **Dashboard**, your central hub for all activity.

---

## 2. The Dashboard Overview

The dashboard gives you a real-time, bird's-eye view of your work.
* **My Tasks:** High-priority tasks assigned explicitly to you.
* **Project Progress:** A quick visual of how close your projects are to completion.
* **Team Activity:** A real-time activity stream of what your team members are currently working on.
* **Upcoming Deadlines:** Urgent deadlines across all projects you are involved in.

*Tip: You can drag and drop widgets to customize your dashboard layout!*

---

## 3. Managing Projects

Projects in ProjectFlow are highly customizable.

### 🔹 Creating a New Project
1. Navigate to **Projects** from the left-hand menu.
2. Click **Create Project**.
3. Choose a template or start from scratch.
4. Set the project visibility:
   - **Private:** Only invited members can see it.
   - **Internal:** Anyone in your organization can see it.
   - **Public:** Fully visible.

### 🔹 Project Views
Depending on your preference, you can view your projects in multiple ways:
- **List View:** Sortable matrix of projects.
- **Grid View:** Card-based overview.
- **Timeline View:** Great for project managers planning milestones and dependencies.
- **Portfolio View:** A multi-project summary tailored for executives.

---

## 4. Task Management & The Kanban Board

Your daily work will mostly involve interacting with Tasks. 

### 🔹 Creating & Managing Tasks
When creating a task, you can specify its:
- **Type:** Feature, Bug, Epic, Subtask.
- **Priority:** Low, Medium, High, Urgent.
- **Due Date & Estimated Hours** for capacity planning.
- **Assignee & Reporter**.

### 🔹 The Kanban Board
Navigate to a project and open the **Board View** to use the Kanban system.
* **Columns** represent the workflow stages (e.g., *To Do → In Progress → Review → Done*).
* **Drag-and-Drop:** Move task cards horizontally across columns to update their status.
* **Swimlanes:** Group tasks horizontally by Assignee or Priority to quickly see who is overloaded.
* **Quick Edit:** Click on any card briefly to update its title or assignee without opening the full detailed view.

---

## 5. Team Collaboration

ProjectFlow is built around real-time communication. You do not need to refresh the page to see updates; everything synchronizes in real-time.

* **Task Comments:** Open any task to leave threaded comments for your team.
* **@Mentions:** Tag a coworker (e.g., `@sarah`) to send them an instant in-app and email notification.
* **Live Updates:** If a teammate is editing a task, you will see a typing indicator.

---

## 6. Time & Workload Management

If your organization utilizes time tracking and capacity planning:
* **Time Entry:** Inside a task, navigate to the `Time Logs` tab to record the hours you’ve worked.
* **Built-in Timer:** Click "Start Timer" when you begin working on a task, and stop it when you take a break or finish.
* **Workload View (For Managers):** Navigating to **Team > Workload** will show a visual representation of task distribution. If a team member is overloaded with hours, the system will highlight them so managers can rebalance the tasks.

---

## 7. Reports & Analytics

Need to report progress to stakeholders?
* Open the **Reports** tab to view Burn-down charts, Velocity charts (team productivity trends), and Resource Utilization metrics.
* Reports can be exported as PDFs or CSVs for easy sharing.
